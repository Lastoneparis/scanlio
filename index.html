Frequently asked questions

Is Scanlio free?

Yes — download and scan for free. A Pro subscription unlocks OCR, PDF editing, e-signature, format conversion and unlimited exports, and removes ads.

Does it work on iPad and Mac?

Yes. Scanlio is a universal app for iPhone, iPad and Mac.

Are my documents private?

Absolutely. Scanning, text recognition and editing are performed on your device. Your documents are never uploaded to our servers.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the App Store → your account → Subscriptions → Scanlio → Cancel. You keep Pro until the end of the period.
