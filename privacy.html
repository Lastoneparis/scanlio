Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Scanlio ("we", "us") is built privacy-first. This policy explains what we do — and mostly don't — with your data.

Your documents stay on your device

All scanning, text recognition (OCR), editing, conversion and storage happen locally on your device. We do not upload, collect, view or store the documents, images or text you create in Scanlio. They never reach our servers.

Data we do not collect

  • We do not require an account, email or sign-in.
  • We do not collect or sell your documents or personal files.

Advertising & analytics (free version)

The free version shows ads via Google AdMob. To serve ads, AdMob may process device identifiers (such as the advertising identifier), approximate location and usage/diagnostic data, in accordance with Google's policies. If you ask the app not to track (App Tracking Transparency), ads are limited to non-personalized. Scanlio Pro removes all ads.

Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Purchases

Subscriptions and purchases are handled by Apple. We never see your payment details. Apple processes transactions per its own privacy policy.

Permissions

  • Camera — to scan documents (only when you scan).
  • Photos — only the images you choose to import or export.
  • Notifications — optional reminders you can turn off anytime.

Children

Scanlio is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be posted here.

Contact
