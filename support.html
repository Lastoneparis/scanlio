Getting started

  • Scan a document: tap the camera button, line up the page — edges are detected automatically — then save. Add more pages to build a multi-page PDF.
  • Import files: use the “+” menu to import a PDF or images (PNG, JPG, HEIC) from Files or Photos.
  • Scan an ID or passport: choose “Scan ID / Passport”, capture the front and back, and it’s saved together. Long-press a document to lock it with Face ID or a PIN.

Editing & converting

  • Edit & sign: open a document → Markup to annotate, add text or sign.
  • Reorder / rotate / delete / merge / split pages: open a document → Pages, or select multiple documents in the library to merge.
  • Convert: open Convert to export a PDF to Word (.rtf), images or text.
  • Share: export as PDF, JPG or Word via Mail, AirDrop, Cloud or any app.

Scanlio Pro & subscriptions

  • Scanlio is free to download and scan. Pro unlocks OCR, PDF editing, e-signature, conversion and unlimited exports, and removes ads.
  • Plans: Weekly, Monthly, or Yearly (with a 3-day free trial). Prices are shown in your local currency at checkout.
  • Restore purchases: Settings → Restore Purchases.
  • Manage / cancel: App Store → your account → Subscriptions → Scanlio. You keep Pro until the end of the current period.

Privacy

Your documents are processed on your device and are never uploaded to our servers. See our Privacy Policy.
